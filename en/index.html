Services

Our Services

Centered on DCS software modification, we provide technical support and related services for plant control and instrumentation.

01

DCS Software Modification & Technical Support

Our core service is DCS (Distributed Control System) software modification, adjustment, and technical support. We also handle supplementary software development and modification related to DCS systems. For plant design, fabrication, or PLC work, we consider each request based on project requirements.

02

Existing System Analysis & Documentation

We analyze existing control systems and create instrument lists, flowcharts, and the specifications needed for modification or upgrade projects. Analysis results can also be applied to operator training materials and operational data visualization.

03

Instrumentation Design & Consulting

We provide instrumentation design for plant facilities and technical consulting for related inquiries.

04

Control & Instrumentation Support

We offer support services related to control and instrumentation work. Depending on scope, we can coordinate with partner companies to meet project needs.

05

Data Center Control Systems (New Initiative)

We are expanding into control systems for data center power, cooling, and facility monitoring. This is a new area where we are applying our industrial control expertise.

Privacy Policy

2. Collection of Personal Information

The Company may collect personal information to the extent necessary for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries

The Company uses collected personal information for the following purposes:

4. Third-Party Provision

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without the individual's consent, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law

  • When cooperating with national or local government agencies in their legally mandated duties, and obtaining consent may impede those duties

The Company maintains the accuracy of personal information and manages it securely, taking necessary and appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, falsification, or leakage.
